New Life

Among her ruins,
Lies a love so precious,
Untainted by any sins,
Now a buried treasure.

It shall remains a lore,
With its hidden secrets,
Discoverable no more
Unearthed only by trail of hints,
Carried through the whisper of the winds..

Her image,
A faint shadow,
Shun by the light,
A silhouette in the night..
Her sound,
A mere whisper,
Silenced by the voices,
A hush among the noises..

Echoed by cries of despair,
No living thing could prepare,
Beckons the tears of anguish,
In grief unknown in language..
Silence of the night,
Seeps within
Sorrow in the heart,
Deep within

Here lies her remains,
Watched by angels above,
On the ground she remains,
Awaiting for a rebirth.

In this palace of saints,
Dwelled her soul's remains
Among the sacred doves,
Where her new life begins.

*In loving memory of my beloved mom. A courageous woman that will always live on in our hearts no matter where she may be right now. May she rest in peace and may the angels in heaven walk with her as she continue on with the journey of her new life.

A Mother's Love

Time that flows
Will never wait
Along with the woes
Brought upon by fate
Buries a love so great
Transcending all boundaries
Forever etched in my memories.

In her warm embrace
I'm never misplaced
Through her tender touch
Eased away all that trouble much
Lingers amidst the gentle breeze
That brushes across my face
Leaving a bitter sweet aftertaste.

Her love
A mother’s love
A love so pure
A precious gift from above
Basking in its grandeur
I smiled
All these while
I knew
Deep inside my heart
It will live on.

© Jean 2012 : May 13th, 2012
