﻿Matchmove/Camera Tracking﻿

﻿﻿Matchmove Showreel

This showreel is a compilation of movies that I've worked on as a Matchmove TD in Rhythm and Hues. My work consists of tracking the live action camera in 3d space, matching the motions and movements of actors using 3d characters as well as assist in building sets and objects that might be necessary for interaction, reflections and shadow casting.

Assisted in the following productions:

-Alvin & the Chipmunks 3:Chipwrecked,
-Chronicles
-Snow White & The Huntsman
-R.I.P.D
-Percy Jackson: Sea of Monsters.

Credits: http://www.imdb.com/name/nm5103726/?ref_=fn_al_nm_1
